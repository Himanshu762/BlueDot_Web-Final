FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Got questions? We’ve answered the ones we hear the most.

How can I volunteer with BlueDot?

We run cleanups, restoration projects, workshops, and more. Contact us to sign up for upcoming events.

Do I need experience or skills to help out?

Not always! Most of our initiatives are beginner-friendly. We’ll provide all necessary orientation and guidance.

We’re a company. Can we partner with you?

Absolutely. From CSR partnerships to employee engagement programs, we offer tailored collaborations. Contact us for further details

Can BlueDot support our CSR goals?

Yes, we co-design nature-positive CSR initiatives that are measurable, transparent, and community-rooted.

How do you measure project success?

We use a mix of ecological data, community feedback, and third-party evaluation to assess real-world impact.

Can I visit your project sites?

Yes, we love showing our work! Visits are welcome with prior scheduling — just drop us a message.

How do I reach your team directly?

You can email us, call us, or use our contact form. We typically respond within 48 hours.
